About Us

Our Telemedicine platform allows you to connect with healthcare providers from the comfort of your home. You can register as a new patient, book appointments, and reschedule as needed, making healthcare more accessible and convenient for you.

Professional healthcare providers available anytime, anywhere.

Easy access to HealthConnect via mobile devices.

Bringing healthcare to underserved areas through mobile connectivity.

Solutions

  • Book appointments and speak with doctors from the comfort of their homes.
  • Access consultations via multiple channels, including USSD, SMS, or online, allowing flexibility no matter where you are.
  • Receive electronic prescriptions directly on their devices, streamlining the treatment process.
  • Benefit from follow-up care without the need for physical visits, ensuring continuous health support.

Our goal is to make healthcare accessible, reliable, and convenient for everyone.

Products

  • 24/7 availability, tailored scheduling for both patients and doctors, and secure data encryption that keeps patient information safe.
  • HealthConnect focuses specifically on underserved regions, using a low-bandwidth mode to ensure smooth consultations even in areas with limited internet access.
  • Patients can also access their health records anytime, providing continuous care even after consultations.
